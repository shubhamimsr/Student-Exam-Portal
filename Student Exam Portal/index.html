About US


INFINITY Tech is a leading Skills and Talent Development Corporation that is building a manpower pool for global industry requirements. INFINITY Tech is a global leader in Skills and Talent Development, offers multi-disciplinary learning management and training delivery solutions to corporations, institutions, and individuals on various technologies. INFINITY Tech has three main lines of business – Corporate Training, Skills and Careers, and School/College Learning.

INFINITY Tech has been founded by a group of highly qualified professionals with an excelled expertise onto varied technological domains as a center for IT education & training with a vision to make candidates ready for IT industry from day one. We believe in in-depth perfection in whatever we do and we strive to bring it alive through INFINITY Tech. INFINITY Tech’s training proved that every candidate from fresher to experience got trained on latest IT requirements and became first choice for industries.

Our highly dedicative staff comes together as a unified team and thrive day and night to achieve the high standards that we have set for ourselves towards your progress. INFINITY Tech understand and believe that just as an organization needs the right talent to drive its business objectives, people need the right environment to grow and achieve their career goals. The moment you step into INFINITY Tech, you would be greeted with the feeling of being at the right place. Along with that, INFINITY Tech affords you with a sense of successful and rewarding career that would be driven by boundless growth opportunities and exposure to cutting-edge technologies and learning possibilities. Each of these elements goes much deeper than what it conveys.

At INFINITY Tech, the focus is more on practical exposure through "Employment” and making every individual well versed with the industry standards. This helps in reducing the time, effort and costs for an organization that looks forward to hire these skilled resources on their training.
